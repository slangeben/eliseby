╰┈☆ bio ☆┈╯

Eliseby is the project of Norwegian artist Elise Bygjordet.

Born in 2004, she feels she was somewhat raised on the internet, a feeling that shines through in her music, where she plays with texture and atmosphere.

Her electronic pop blends into artpop, drawing inspiration from artists such as Caroline Polachek, Becky and the Birds, and Emilie Nicolas.

Her debut EP, Djevels Datter, introduces us to the world of Eliseby, where religious trauma, love, and dysfunction form the heart of her sound.

She hopes her music can bring others the same sense of escape she found in going online or producing.
